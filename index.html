About the Author

The voice
behind the verses.

Rachel DCruz is a poet who writes from the heart — transforming raw emotion into powerful, unflinching verses. Her work explores love, longing, healing, and self-discovery, often drawing from personal experience and the kind of quiet observations most people are afraid to say out loud.

With a voice that is both vulnerable and bold, Rachel's poetry invites readers to feel deeply and see themselves reflected in her words. She writes for the ones who have loved too hard, grieved too quietly, and rebuilt themselves when no one was watching.

Her debut collection, Every poem was about you, is available now.
